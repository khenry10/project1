
Bronze MVP: 1 user clicks to find matches and wins when he synchronously selects matched pairs. (minimal to no styling)

User Stories;
[] As a user, I can't see the pairs until I click on one
[] As a user, I get points when I find matched pairs
[] As a user, I win when when I find all matched pairs
[] As a user, the game resets with I win
[] As a user, the pairs are in different places when the game resets
